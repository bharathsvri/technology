# Pair Programming and Mobbing

**Pair programming** (two engineers, one workstation) and **mobbing** (whole team rotating driver) improve **knowledge spread** and **code quality** when used intentionally—not as infinite meetings.

---

## Effective pairing

- **Rotate** driver/navigator roles **often** (25–30 minutes).
- **Navigator** thinks strategically; **driver** types—avoid **silent** pairing.
- **Time-box** for exploratory spikes vs **deep** implementation sessions.

---

## Mobbing when it helps

- **Complex** refactors touching many modules.
- **Onboarding** new hires to codebase conventions.

---

## Anti-patterns

- **All-day** pairing without breaks → burnout.
- **Senior-only** keyboard—defeats learning goals.
