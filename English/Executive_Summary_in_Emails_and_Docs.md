# Executive Summaries for Engineers

Leaders scan **BLUF** (**bottom line up front**): **what** you need, **risk**, **next step**—then optional detail.

---

## Template (five sentences max)

1. **Context** in one line (“We’re rolling out **v2 auth**”).
2. **Current status** (“**90%** migrated; **one** tenant blocked”).
3. **Risk** (“**Revenue** reports delayed **1 day** if not unblocked”).
4. **Ask** (“Approve **temporary** read-only mode **OR** assign owner for hotfix”).
5. **Timeline** (“Decision needed **today** to avoid weekend work”).

---

## Attachments

Put **charts after** the summary—some readers never open files.
