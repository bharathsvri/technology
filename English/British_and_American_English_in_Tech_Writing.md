# British vs American English in Global Tech Teams

Spellings and vocabulary differ (**colour/color**, **licence/license** noun). For **APIs** and **code**, **consistency** beats regional preference—pick one **style guide** per repo.

---

## Code and identifiers

- **US spelling** dominates many OSS ecosystems (`initialize`, `color` in CSS).
- **Do not** mix spellings for **public** API field names across versions.

---

## Documentation

Match **customer-facing** locale expectations or write **neutral international English**.

---

## Dates and numbers

Prefer **ISO 8601** dates in writing; clarify **timezone** for calls.
