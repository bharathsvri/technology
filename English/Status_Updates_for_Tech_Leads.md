# Status Updates for Tech Leads

Leads need **signal** not noise: **RAG** (Red/Amber/Green) on commitments, **blockers with owner**, and **forecast** changes early.

---

## Weekly template

- **Shipped**: bullets tied to **tickets/releases**.
- **In flight**: **ETA** + confidence (**high/medium/low**).
- **Risks**: dependency, **capacity**, **defect** trends.
- **Asks**: **one** decision or **resource** request per week max.

---

## Avoid

- **Copy-paste** of every team member’s daily standup—**aggregate** themes.
