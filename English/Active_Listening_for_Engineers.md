# Active Listening for Engineers

**Active listening** means confirming you understood before solving—critical in **incident bridges** and **requirements** calls.

---

## Techniques

- **Paraphrase**: “So the failure is **only** on **EU** pods after **12 UTC**, right?”
- **Label emotion** sparingly: “Sounds **frustrating**—let’s capture timeline next.”
- **Ask clarifying questions** before proposing architecture.

---

## Remote specifics

- **Pause** longer on video latency; use **raised hand** norms.
