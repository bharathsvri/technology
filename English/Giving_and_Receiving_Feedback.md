# Giving and Receiving Feedback

Engineering careers advance on **specific, actionable** feedback—not vague praise or personality labels.

---

## Giving feedback (SBI-style)

- **Situation**: when/where (“in yesterday’s design review”).
- **Behavior**: observable (“you interrupted twice before others finished”).
- **Impact**: effect on team (“we missed the scaling concern until the end”).

Ask permission if culture requires it: **“Five minutes of feedback?”**

---

## Receiving feedback

- **Listen fully**, then **paraphrase** to confirm understanding.
- **Separate identity from behavior** — “I missed tests” not “I am careless.”
- **Choose experiments**: one **concrete** change for next sprint.

---

## Code review feedback

- **Nit vs blocker** labels; **suggest** don’t command unless compliance/security.
- **Praise in public**, **coaching in private** for repeated patterns.
