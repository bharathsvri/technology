# One-on-Ones with Your Manager

**1:1s** are recurring time to **align priorities**, **surface risks**, and **get feedback**—not status-only meetings if used well.

---

## Bring an agenda

- **Top 3** topics: wins, blockers, career.
- **Share early** (doc or message) so your manager can prep.

---

## Status vs strategic use

- **Slack** can cover daily **status**; use 1:1 for **decisions**, **trade-offs**, and **growth**.

---

## Ask for feedback explicitly

“What should I **start/stop/continue**?” once a month—reduces guesswork.
