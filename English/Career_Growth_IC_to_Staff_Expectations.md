# Career Growth: IC Expectations Toward Staff

**Staff+** impact is measured in **multi-team outcomes**, **technical direction**, and **sustainable** systems—not only individual **throughput**.

---

## Mid → Senior signals

- **Owns** features end-to-end: design, rollout, metrics, **postmortems**.
- **Mentors** via reviews and pairing without **gatekeeping**.

---

## Senior → Staff signals

- **Defines** technical strategy for a **domain** (API standards, data models, SLOs).
- **Aligns** engineering with **product** and **security** early.
- **Writes** ADRs/RFCs that others actually **use**.

---

## Pitfalls

- **IC forever** myth: leadership of **technical** decisions ≠ people management track.
- **Heroics** without **documentation** do not scale your **leverage**.
