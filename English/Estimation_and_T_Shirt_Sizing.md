# Estimation and T-Shirt Sizing for Engineers

**Estimation** is not fortune-telling—it is **communicating uncertainty** and **de-risking** work so stakeholders can plan.

---

## T-shirt sizes (S/M/L/XL)

Map to **ranges**, not fake precision:

- **S**: hours to a day; known path.
- **M**: a few days; some unknowns.
- **L**: week+; needs spike or design.
- **XL**: split the work—**too large to commit**.

Pair sizes with **assumptions** and **dependencies**.

---

## Spikes

Time-box **discovery** (e.g. “2 days to prove Kafka retention policy”) before committing **delivery** dates.

---

## Buffers

Add explicit **risk buffer** for integrations, security review, or flaky CI—**separate line item** so it is not silently eaten.
