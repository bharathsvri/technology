# Slack and Async Written Communication

**Slack** (or Teams chat) is **high-bandwidth, low-structure**—great for **quick** alignment, dangerous for **binding** decisions without a durable link.

---

## Message hygiene

- **First line** = request + deadline (“**Need approval by 4pm UTC** …”).
- **Thread** replies to keep **channel** readable.
- **`@here` / `@channel`** sparingly—noise erodes trust.

---

## When to move out of chat

- **Architecture** decisions → **ADR** or design doc.
- **Customer commitments** → ticket + email summary.

---

## Searchability

Prefer **keywords** your future self will grep: **service name**, **error code**, **PR link**.
