# Stakeholder Communication for Individual Contributors

Even without direct reports, **ICs** influence outcomes through **PMs**, **designers**, **SREs**, and **customers**—communication is the multiplier.

---

## Clarify roles

- **RACI-lite**: know if you are **Responsible**, **Accountable**, **Consulted**, or **Informed** for a decision.
- **Escalate early** on **scope** or **security** blockers—silent slips become crises.

---

## Translate technical risk

Use **impact language**: “If we skip load tests, **checkout** may degrade during **sale** weekend” beats “we might have latency.”

Offer **two** options with **trade-offs**, not endless exploration.

---

## Status that helps

- **Done / doing / blocked** with **next action owner** and **date**.
- **No surprises**: surface **yellow** flags before they turn **red**.
